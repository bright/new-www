Easy Card

Easy Card is an application for collecting credit card payments on mobile phone (iOS and Android). The main challenge that Bright Inventions took and successfully overcame was to integrate with various custom hardware, including magnetic card swipers.

  • scanning magnetic credit cards
  • scanning credit cards using device camera
  • collecting electronic signature
  • generating receipts and sending them via email
  • support for both left-to-right and right-to-left UI orientations
